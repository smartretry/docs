## Integration & compatibility

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How can I integrate SmartRetry with my system?">
    SmartRetry offers multiple integration options — a **REST API**, **SDKs**, and ready-made **plugins** for popular platforms like WooCommerce and others. You can choose the method that best fits your technical stack, and SmartRetry's team will assist you during setup to ensure a smooth and fast integration.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does the integration process take?">
    The integration process usually takes between **2 to 5 business days**, depending on your platform and setup. Using the official SmartRetry plugin for platforms like WooCommerce, Magento, or PrestaShop can significantly speed up the process.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a sandbox or testing environment?">
    Yes. You can start with a **sandbox environment** that replicates the full production flow. It allows your developers to test API calls, webhooks, and response handling before going live.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can SmartRetry connect directly to my payment provider?">
    Yes, in many cases SmartRetry can **connect directly to your existing payment provider**, and in some instances this connection is already supported. This means integration can be done with **no additional development work** on your side — you simply need to **request the activation** from your provider.

    However, if you work with **multiple providers**, it is generally recommended to keep SmartRetry integrated on your side rather than through a single provider, since providers typically have an incentive to keep transactions within their own network. SmartRetry's team can advise you on the optimal setup for your specific use case.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does SmartRetry support popular eCommerce platforms?">
    Yes. SmartRetry provides **plugins and SDKs** that work with leading eCommerce platforms such as **WooCommerce, Magento, and PrestaShop**, making it easy to connect without custom development. Additional integrations are added continuously to support a wide range of payment environments and online stores.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does SmartRetry support Shopify, Wix Payments, or other closed platforms?">
    Support for **Shopify, Wix Payments**, and other closed or restricted platforms may vary depending on their current API and plugin policies. In many cases, partial or indirect integration is still possible — for example, through your payment provider's API.

    <Note>Contact SmartRetry directly to check the available options for your specific platform setup.</Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use webhooks to create new transactions?">
    No. Webhooks in SmartRetry are used **only for status updates and reporting**, not for initiating new transactions. New transactions are always created through the **API, SDK, or plugin**, ensuring data consistency and security.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does SmartRetry have access to card details?">
    Yes. SmartRetry operates with access to **card details and transaction data** as part of the retry and optimization process. All sensitive data is handled securely and never exposed to unauthorized parties. SmartRetry follows strict encryption and privacy standards throughout the transaction lifecycle.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pricing & billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How is SmartRetry pricing structured?">
    SmartRetry uses a **performance-based pricing model** — you pay **only for successful transactions** that SmartRetry helped approve. In most cases, there is **no charge for declined or failed retries**, meaning SmartRetry earns only when you do.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When will I receive my invoice?">
    Invoices are issued on a **weekly or monthly basis**, depending on your commercial agreement. Each invoice includes a summary of processed and successfully approved transactions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get a discount for higher volumes?">
    Yes. Clients who commit to a **minimum monthly transaction volume** for a 12-month period receive **preferential rates**. The more successful transactions SmartRetry processes for you, the lower your cost per transaction becomes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel at any time?">
    Yes — you can **cancel anytime with no long-term commitment**. If you haven't received a dedicated discount, you can simply stop sending transactions through SmartRetry, and no further charges will apply.

    If you received a **volume-based discount or annual commitment**, you'll need to refund the value of the discount before termination. You are billed only for transactions successfully processed through the system.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a minimum volume requirement?">
    Minimum volume requirements — if any — are determined by your **sales representative** and depend on your business size, average transaction count, and processing setup.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
